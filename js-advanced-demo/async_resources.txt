[PROMISES]
Since async functions are essentially a promise in a nice wrapper, understanding the general idea of promises 
is still very important.

Mozilla intro: https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Guide/Using_promises

Here is a walkthrough on the distinction between promises and async/await: 
https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/difference-between-promise-and-async-await-in-node-js/

And some other general information on promises...
W3schools intro: https://www.w3schools.com/js/js_promise.asp
More Mozilla documentation: https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/Promise

Asynchronous JavaScript tutorial on promises: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=slIJj-zbs_M 

GeeksForGeeks walkthrough: https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/javascript-promises/
(This walkthrough explains the different states of promises, which may be useful to anyone new to promises and async)
